Chinese Broccoli

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch gai lan (Chinese broccoli), trimmed
  • 3 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook Chinese broccoli in boiling water, uncovered, until just tender, about 4 minutes.

Step 3

Drain and place in a shallow bowl.

Step 4

Whisk together hoisin sauce, sugar, soy sauce, cornstarch, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger in a small saucepan over medium heat until sauce is thickened and no longer cloudy, 5 to 7 minutes.
